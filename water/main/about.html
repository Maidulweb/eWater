About aquadeli®

aquadeli is New Zealand’s first concept store for water, trading since 2006. We stock New Zealand’s largest range of mineral and spring waters, books about water and water accessories. We specialise in sourcing and supplying leading ‘source of origin™’ waters. These may be classics like Vichy Catalan, or contemporary brands such as Antipodes.

All these waters have one thing in common. They are bottled at source and labelled to show their origin, much like Champagne can only come from one region. Otherwise, just like us, each is unique. Some emerge from their source highly mineralised and naturally carbonated, while others may be almost devoid of mineral content.

For ten years we had a physical store located on Mt Eden Road, Auckland. But as most of our clients now purchase online we have moved to a dedicated online store. We deliver to home and office and welcome orders from businesses seeking something to compliment their existing range of beverages for staff or clients.
